Chicken curry with basmati rice
Cooking time: 30 minutes Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients:

2

Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned.

2. Add the curry paste and cook for 1 minute.

3. Stir in the coconut milk and bring to a simmer.

4. Reduce the heat and cook for 10 minutes.

5. Serve with basmati rice.
